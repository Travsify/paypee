1. Intended Use & Crypto Integration

Paypee is a next-generation financial operating system designed to bridge the gap between traditional fiat currencies and digital assets for African businesses and individuals.

Intended Use of Services

Paypee intends to use the services provided by Bitnob and Quidax to enhance its institutional liquidity and settle cross-border transactions efficiently. Our use case focuses on:

  • Cross-Border Remittance: Utilizing stablecoin liquidity to settle transactions between Nigeria and global markets instantly.
  • Treasury Management: Holding and managing corporate assets in USD-pegged stablecoins to mitigate local currency volatility.
  • Virtual Card Funding: Providing a mechanism for users to fund their USD virtual cards using crypto-to-fiat conversion layers.

Crypto Functionalities

  • Stablecoin Settlement: Conversion between NGN and USDT/USDC for institutional settlement.
  • Bitcoin Lightning: Leveraging Bitnob infrastructure for global micro-payments.
  • MPC Custody: High-security digital asset management through regulated partners.

2. User Journey Overview

Stage 1: Onboarding

Users undergo a 3-layer verification process:

  • Email & Phone: Initial contact point verification.
  • Identity Verification: Automated query against NIN/Passport/CAC databases via Prembly.
  • Biometric Match: Real-time facial liveness capture compared against ID data.

Stage 2: Funding & Infrastructure

Upon verification, users are issued a dedicated NGN virtual account via Fincra. Funds can be added via traditional bank transfers or international receipts.

Stage 3: Transactions

Users access the multi-currency dashboard to perform FX swaps, issue USD virtual cards, and initiate global payouts to 20+ countries.

3. Service Delivery Strategy

Operational Posture

Paypee is currently in a Controlled Beta phase (Stealth Mode). We onboard users through:

  • Directed Invitation: Vetted partners and institutional clients.
  • API-First Delivery: Infrastructure-as-a-Service for B2B developers.
  • Private Dashboard: Secure web-based React interface accessible via authenticated partner portals.

This approach ensure 100% compliance during the initial launch phase before full public scaling in Q3 2026.

4. AML/CFT Policy Summary

Paypee complies with CBN AML/CFT Regulations 2022 and FATF recommendations.

Key Controls

  • Sanctions Screening: Every transaction is screened against OFAC, UN, and local PEP lists.
  • Transaction Monitoring: Automated detection of velocity spikes and structuring attempts.
  • Reporting: Suspicious Transaction Reports (STR) are filed with the NFIU within 24 hours.

5. Support & Disputes

Customer Success

Paypee provides 24/7 support via in-app live chat and email (support@paypee.co).

Resolution Protocols

  • Failed Transfers: Reconciled automatically on T+1 basis.
  • Card Chargebacks: Handled via Visa/Mastercard scheme rules through our partner, Bridgecard.
  • Escalation: Level 3 issues (compliance/security) are resolved within 48 hours.

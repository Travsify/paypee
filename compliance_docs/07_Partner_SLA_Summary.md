# PARTNER INTEGRATION & SERVICE PROVISION SUMMARY

**Entity:** Pickpadi Global Inclusive Limited  
**Project:** Paypee Infrastructure  

---

## 1. KYC CAPABILITIES: PREMBLY (IDENTITY PASS)
**Provider:** Prembly (Formerly Identity Pass)  
**Status:** Active Service Level Agreement (SLA)  
**Description:** Paypee has integrated Prembly's biometric and document verification APIs. This partnership covers:
*   NIN (National Identity Number) Real-time Validation.
*   BVN (Bank Verification Number) biometric matching.
*   CAC (Corporate Affairs Commission) business registry lookups.
*   **Clarification:** Prembly is the parent company of the Identity Pass product. All technical calls are routed through the `api.prembly.com` endpoint.

## 2. BANKING & RAILS: FINCRA
**Provider:** Fincra Technologies Limited  
**Status:** Live Production Agreement  
**Description:** Paypee utilizes Fincra as its primary NGN/USD banking infrastructure partner for:
*   Issuance of virtual account numbers (NUBAN).
*   Fiat payouts and settlements.
*   Virtual debit card issuance (Mastercard/Visa).

## 3. LIQUIDITY & CROSS-BORDER: BITNOB
**Provider:** Bitnob Technologies Ltd  
**Status:** Institutional API Onboarding  
**Description:** Paypee leverages Bitnob for:
*   Institutional stablecoin liquidity (USDT/USDC).
*   Bitcoin Lightning Network settlement for instant micro-remittances.

## 4. SECONDARY CARD PROVIDER: BRIDGECARD
**Provider:** Bridgecard (Cards-as-a-Service)  
**Status:** Active Integration  
**Description:** Provides failover redundancy for virtual card issuance and global spend limits.

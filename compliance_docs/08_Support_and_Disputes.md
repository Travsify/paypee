# Customer Support & Dispute Resolution Framework

**Topic:** Fund Safety, Support Channels, and Resolution Protocols  
**Date:** April 2026

## 1. Support Infrastructure
Paypee maintains a dedicated support layer to handle user inquiries and technical issues:
*   **Support Channels:** 
    *   In-app Live Chat (24/7 responsiveness).
    *   Email Support: `support@paypee.co`.
    *   Direct Relationship Manager (for Business & Developer accounts).
*   **Self-Service:** Comprehensive documentation (Docs.tsx) and AI Advisor (AiAdvisor.tsx) to handle common FAQs.

## 2. Dispute Resolution Protocol
In the event of a transaction discrepancy, the following steps apply:

### 2.1 Payouts & Transfers
*   **Resolution Window:** Disputes must be logged within 7 days of the transaction.
*   **T+1 Reconciliation:** For failed NGN transfers, automated reconciliation attempts occur within 24 hours.
*   **Manual Intervention:** If automated reconciliation fails, the compliance team initiates a manual traceback with the partner bank.

### 2.2 Card Transactions
*   **Fraud Flagging:** Users can instantly freeze cards via the dashboard.
*   **Chargebacks:** Handled in accordance with Visa/Mastercard scheme rules through our card issuer partner. Resolution times typically range from 15 to 45 days.

### 2.3 Account Access & Recovery
*   **Locked Accounts:** Accounts flagged for security (e.g., suspicious IP change) require the user to undergo a secondary "Liveness Match" (via Prembly) to regain access.
*   **Fund Recovery:** In case of lost credentials, fund recovery is processed after verifying Government ID and 2FA contact points.

## 3. Escalation Path
1.  **Level 1:** Automated Support / Chat Agent.
2.  **Level 2:** Human Support Specialist (Target Response: 2 hours).
3.  **Level 3:** Compliance/Operations Manager (Target Resolution: 48 hours).

## 4. Fund Withdrawal Security
To prevent unauthorized fund withdrawal:
*   **MFA:** Mandatory 2FA for all outgoing transfers.
*   **Cooling Period:** 24-hour withdrawal freeze following a primary security change (e.g., Password Reset).
*   **Manual Review:** Transfers exceeding specific institutional thresholds require manual "Second Look" approval by the operations lead.

## 1. STATEMENT OF COMMITMENT
Pickpadi Global Inclusive Limited ("The Company") is committed to high standards of Anti-Money Laundering and Countering the Financing of Terrorism (AML/CFT) compliance. We operate in strict accordance with the Money Laundering (Prevention and Prohibition) Act 2022 and international FATF recommendations.

## 2. RISK-BASED APPROACH (RBA)
Paypee employs an RBA to identify, assess, and understand the money laundering and terrorist financing risks to which it is exposed.
*   **Customer Risk:** Evaluated based on identity, occupation, and source of funds.
*   **Product Risk:** Higher scrutiny for cross-border stablecoin transfers and virtual card payouts.
*   **Geographic Risk:** Enhanced monitoring for transactions involving high-risk jurisdictions.

## 3. CUSTOMER DUE DILIGENCE (CDD) & KYC
We operate a three-tier KYC structure via our partner **Prembly (Identity Pass)**:
*   **Tier 1 (Basic):** Phone and Name verification (Low limits).
*   **Tier 2 (Standard):** Government ID (NIN/BVN) verification.
*   **Tier 3 (Enhanced):** Biometric Liveness check and Proof of Address.

## 4. TRANSACTION MONITORING (TM)
Our system monitors transactions for:
*   **Structuring (Smurfing):** Repeated small transactions to avoid reporting thresholds.
*   **Velocity Checks:** Unusual frequency of transactions within a 24-hour window.
*   **Sanctions Screening:** Real-time screening against OFAC, UN, and local consolidated lists.

## 5. SUSPICIOUS ACTIVITY REPORTING (SAR)
The Compliance Officer (Patrick Achua) is responsible for filing SARs with the Nigerian Financial Intelligence Unit (NFIU) within required statutory timelines whenever a transaction is deemed suspicious.

## 6. RECORD KEEPING
The Company maintains all customer identification data and transaction records for a minimum of five (5) years following the termination of the business relationship.

## 7. EMPLOYEE TRAINING
All staff undergo mandatory annual AML/CFT training to identify red flags and understand internal escalation protocols.

## 8. INDEPENDENT AUDIT
The AML/CFT program is subject to an annual independent review by an external compliance audit firm to ensure effectiveness.

# Paypee User Journey Overview

**Entity:** Pickpadi Global Inclusive Limited (RC: 8229347)  
**Compliance Officer:** Patrick Achua  
**Date:** April 2026

## 1. Onboarding & Registration
1.  **Installation/Entry:** Users enter through the Paypee platform (currently accessible via direct invitation or partner dashboard).
2.  **Account Creation:** Users provide basic details (Email, Phone, Password).
3.  **Role Selection:** Users choose between **Individual**, **Business**, or **Developer** accounts.
4.  **KYC Verification (The "Verification Gate"):**
    *   **Layer 1:** Phone and Email verification.
    *   **Layer 2:** Identity verification via **Prembly (IdentityPass)**.
    *   **Layer 3:** Biometric Liveness Match. A real-time selfie is captured and compared against the government-issued ID database (NIN/Passport/CAC).
5.  **Approval:** Upon successful verification (usually <60 seconds), the account is activated and virtual infrastructure is provisioned.

## 2. Service Access & Account Funding
1.  **Virtual Accounts:** Upon activation, a dedicated NGN virtual account is automatically provisioned via **Fincra**.
2.  **Funding:**
    *   **Bank Transfer:** Users fund their wallet via traditional bank transfer to their virtual account.
    *   **Global Receipts:** Businesses can receive USD/EUR/GBP which is converted and settled in their local wallet.
3.  **Wallet Management:** Users can view their balances across different currency "Vaults".

## 3. High-Fidelity Transactions
1.  **Global Payouts:** Users can initiate transfers to local bank accounts or international accounts through the FX swap interface.
2.  **Virtual Cards:**
    *   Users can issue instant USD virtual cards for global online spending.
    *   Cards are funded directly from the Paypee wallet.
3.  **Bill Payments:** Access to utility payments, airtime, and data through the Bills Dashboard.
4.  **Treasury Vaults:** Businesses can lock funds in yield-bearing or stablecoin-backed vaults.

## 4. Withdrawals & Off-boarding
1.  **Fiat Withdrawal:** Users can withdraw NGN directly to any Nigerian bank.
2.  **Crypto Exit (Institutional):** For verified businesses, withdrawals can be settled in stablecoins to designated corporate wallets via the Quidax/Bitnob bridge.
3.  **Security Measures:** Every withdrawal requires 2FA (Multi-factor Authentication) and is subject to tiered transaction limits based on KYC level.

---
**Summary of Flow:**
`Onboard (KYC) -> Fund (Bank Transfer) -> Transact (FX/Cards) -> Withdraw (Local Bank)`

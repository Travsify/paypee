# Third-Party Partnership Details

**Report Date:** April 2026

Paypee relies on a robust ecosystem of regulated partners to deliver various financial services. Below is the breakdown of our strategic integrations:

## 1. Banking & Collections Partner
*   **Name:** **Fincra** (Moneymie)
*   **Primary Purpose:** Virtual Account Provisioning (NGN), Payout Rails, and Wallet Infrastructure.
*   **Integration:** REST API via `/api/virtual-accounts`.
*   **Regulation:** Regulated as a Payment Service Provider (PSP).

## 2. Crypto Liquidity & Infrastructure
*   **Name:** **Quidax**
*   **Primary Purpose:** Stablecoin liquidity (USDT/USDC) for institutional settlement and cross-border FX.
*   **Integration:** Institutional API.
*   **Regulation:** Registered Digital Asset Exchange.

*   **Name:** **Bitnob**
*   **Primary Purpose:** Bitcoin Lightning Network settlement and USD stablecoin rails for international transfers.
*   **Integration:** Bitnob Enterprise API.

## 3. KYC & Identity Verification
*   **Name:** **Prembly (IdentityPass)**
*   **Primary Purpose:** Government database queries (NIN/BVN), Business vetting (CAC), and AI-driven liveness checks.
*   **Integration:** Mobile/Web SDK with server-side validation.
*   **Regulation:** Authorized ID validation partner.

## 4. Cards Infrastructure
*   **Name:** Fincra / Bridgecard
*   **Primary Purpose:** Issuance and management of USD/NGN Virtual Cards.
*   **Integration:** Card-as-a-Service (CaaS) API.

## 5. Technology Providers
*   **Cloud Architecture:** Amazon Web Services (AWS) - GDPR compliant.
*   **Monitoring:** Datadog / Sentry for real-time security alerts.
